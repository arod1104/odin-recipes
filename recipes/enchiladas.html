Delicious Chicken Enchiladas

A tasty Mexican dish that's easy to make and full of flavor!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 1 (10 oz) can enchilada sauce
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 8 (6 inch) corn tortillas
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/2 cup sour cream (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a medium bowl, combine the shredded chicken, half of the enchilada sauce, 1/2 cup of cheddar cheese, 1/2 cup of Monterey Jack cheese, and chopped onion.
  3. Spread a thin layer of the remaining enchilada sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  4. Place about 1/3 cup of the chicken mixture in the center of each tortilla, roll it up, and place seam-side down in the baking dish.
  5. Pour the remaining enchilada sauce over the top of the rolled tortillas.
  6. Sprinkle the remaining cheddar and Monterey Jack cheese over the top.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Garnish with chopped fresh cilantro and serve with sour cream if desired. Enjoy!
